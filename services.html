  What works can we do?

✧✧

Our company has global projects experience in power substation testing & commissioning, and systems. We have vast experience with oil & gas, solar & wind farms,  utilities, data centres and the rail industry.  Engineering-shed can perform FAT, SAT, Commissioning tests for power systems of 11 kV, 33kV,  &&13.8kV, 34.5kV Maintenance statutory testing for 11 & 33kV substations, switchgear testing and upgrades and other HV work. and protection scheme testing. Switchgear testing including:

1) Perform insulation Resistance Tests on Each Bus Section.  2) Perform high pot HV switchgear tests. 3) Perform Phasing Checks on double ended switchgear to ensure correct phasing from each source. 4) Energization of power substations up to 110kV. 5)Protection Schemes, CT, VT, phasing, substation earthing.  6) G59 application & commissioning.  DNO applications, EPC     & Following Up...we shall conclude by:-

Upon completion of testing and commissioning we shall show by demonstration in service test that switchgear is performing the intended function - HV Circuit breakers shall be tripped by operation of each protective device. Please note that we are authorised and regulated for high voltage work and posses the necessary engineering licence for such works.
