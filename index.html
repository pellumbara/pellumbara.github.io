Substation Testing & Commissioning

EngineeringShed is a freelance business and was founded in 2008 under the name of DriniElektrik  to fulfilling the need of the construction industry.  It has since evolved, advanced, restructured and is now more dedicated to substation testing & commissioning of switch gear and protection schemes performing design, installations, testing and commissioning through to project energization and handover.  Our 16 + years experience with oil & gas, solar and wind farms, factories, data centres and the utilities is a guarantee for project success.  We are happy to help.  Give us a buzz and see how easy the work shall fly!
